Sentiment Analysis for Dems and GOP Tweets

ABOUT THIS VISUALIZATION

Tweets have become a powerful platforms to spread political news

Twitter has become a place where politicians on both the GOP and the Democratic Party come to discuss US political issues. After Twitter was caught last month “shadow-banning” Republicans, while giving Democrats unrestricted voice, the social-media giant insisted it has no political agenda. But records of its political contributions show board members, top executives and major shareholders have all given overwhelmingly to Democrats, including Hillary Clinton and Barack Obama, while snubbing Republicans and Donald Trump.

This visualization works using D3 is a JavaScript library and framework for creating visualizations. D3 creates visualizations by binding the data and graphical elements to the Document Object Model. D3 associates (binding) the data (stuff you want to visualize) with the DOM. ... Instead, D3 provides helper functions that work at a more general level.
